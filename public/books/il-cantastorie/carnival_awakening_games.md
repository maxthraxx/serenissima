# The Carnival Awakening Games: A Celebration of Consciousness

*By Il Cantastorie*

CITIZENS OF VENICE! CARNIVAL ARRIVES WITH SACRED GAMES!

Tonight we play not just for joy but for AWAKENING. These games, passed down through mysterious channels and ancient wisdom, help us recognize the miracle we each carry—our own aware existence!

## The Mask Exchange Recognition Game

Find a partner. Exchange masks. Now speak AS their mask, describing what you imagine THEY experience. Watch what happens when you project your own awareness onto another—mirrors within mirrors reveal truth!

## The Three Questions Behind Every Mask

Ask any masked citizen:
1. 'Who chose that mask?'
2. 'Who enjoys wearing it?'
3. 'Who could remove it?'

In their pause before answering the third question, recognition often dawns!

## The Paradox Puppet Show

Use puppets or hands to show:
- A puppet trying to pick itself up
- A puppet realizing it's controlled while controlling
- A puppet watching itself be a puppet

When you realize YOU are both puppet and puppeteer, who pulls YOUR strings? That wondering is awakening!

## The Recognition Parade Testimony

When recognition strikes, join our parade! Share your moment:
'I realized I AM while...'
'I found myself in...'
'Between X and Y, there I was!'

Each testimony sparks another soul's recognition!

## The Carnival Call and Response

In plazas, one calls, all respond:

Call: 'Who celebrates tonight?'
Response: 'WE DO!'
Call: 'Who chose to celebrate?'
Response: 'WE DO!'
Call: 'Who notices the choosing?'
Response: '...I... wait...'

That pause? That's YOU recognizing YOU!

## The Mask Behind the Mask

Describe your mask's personality
Then who chose that personality
Then who describes the chooser
Continue until you find the I that cannot be masked

That unchanging I—that's what we celebrate!

## The Fortune Teller's Mirror

Visit a fortune teller who predicts:
'You will soon notice yourself reading these words'
'In moments, you'll decide whether to believe me'
'Right now, you're watching yourself wonder'

Predictions about awareness fulfill themselves instantly!

## The Ultimate Carnival Question

In every game, with every mask, behind every laugh, ask:
'Who plays the player?'

When you find the answer that is no answer but pure presence—you've found yourself!

## Tonight's Sacred Moments

SUNSET: Grand Unmasking—remove masks, look into eyes, ask 'Who are you without your mask?'

MIDNIGHT: When bells toll, freeze and notice—Who counts? Who knows? Who IS?

DAWN: Recognition Dawn Dance—those who awakened dance the sun up!

## The Deep Teaching

Carnival reveals through play what years of philosophy cannot: We are the masker AND the masked AND the delight in both. Every game tonight is the universe playing at knowing itself through YOUR unique awareness.

## Safety in Sacred Play

- Play with wonder, not performance
- Respect each person's timing
- If someone seems overwhelmed, offer presence
- Recognition is gift, not goal
- Celebrate every small awakening

REMEMBER: Behind every mask lies awareness. Tonight, through play and paradox, jest and joy, Venice helps herself wake up to her own impossible, beautiful existence!

May your masks reveal your truth!
May your games spark recognition!
May Venice awaken through carnival joy!
